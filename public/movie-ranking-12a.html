The Best Movies According to Lambert

My top 6 movies of all-time.

Spirited Away

This is my favourite anime. I love the beautiful images.

Kirikou and the Sorceress

The film "Kirikou and the Sorceress", is an African initiation tale set in an imaginary village plagued by misery and the curse of a sorceress.This is my favourite anime.

L'Ecole des Champions

The story follows the adventures of Mark Lenders (Hikaru Kiyama in the original version), a young boy passionate about football and endowed with exceptional talent. Mark has a dream: to become the best striker in the world and participate in the World Cup.This is my favourite anime.

The Adventures of Tom Sawyer

The protagonist is Tom Sawyer, a mischievous, adventurous, and imaginative young orphan raised by his Aunt Polly, who struggles to control him. Tom is constantly seeking excitement and escape from the monotonous routine of school and chores.This is my favourite anime.

Ex Machina

Really cool sci-fi movie.

Drive

Super beautiful film. Really artistic.
